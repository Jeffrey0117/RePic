虛擬相簿 — 這功能超扯

做著做著，我搞出了一個連我自己都覺得扯的功能 — 虛擬相簿。

想像一下：你在 Twitter 看到一張喜歡的圖，你把它拖進 RePic。

RePic 不會下載那張圖。它會建立一個 .repic 檔案。

這個檔案有多大？

<1 KB

裡面就是一個 JSON，存了圖片的 URL 跟裁切參數。但你雙擊它，就能直接看到那張圖，還能裁切、標註。

等於說你可以用「幾乎零空間」收藏無限張網路圖片。

這不是超神？我自己做完都覺得不可思議。一個資料夾放一萬個 .repic 檔案，佔不到 10 MB。但你擁有一萬張圖片的即時存取權。

不只是虛擬相簿

RePic 當然不只有虛擬相簿：

  • ⚡ 極速瀏覽 — 虛擬捲動 + 縮圖預載，幾千張圖滑起來跟原生一樣順
  • 🌐 網頁相簿 — 從任何網站拖圖片進來，自動繞過防盜連
  • ✂️ 非破壞性編輯 — 裁切、標註、馬賽克，原圖完全不動
  • 📋 剪貼簿整合 — Ctrl+V 貼上截圖直接進相簿
  • 🖱️ 拖曳分享 — 直接拖到 Discord、LINE 就能傳
  • 🌙 暗色 / 亮色主題 — 你的眼睛你做主

Let me be honest: every image viewer I've tried has let me down.

Windows Photos takes forever to open a single image. Third-party viewers either look like they're from 2005, charge a subscription, or stuff in features nobody asked for.

I'm a developer who works with images every day. One day I finally snapped and decided to build my own.

That's how RePic was born.

Virtual Albums — the feature that blew my own mind

While building RePic, I stumbled onto a feature that even surprised me — Virtual Albums.

Here's how it works: you see an image on Twitter that you like. You drag it into RePic.

RePic doesn't download that image. Instead, it creates a .repic file.

How big is this file?

<1 KB

It's just a JSON file with the image URL and optional crop parameters. But double-click it, and you see the full image instantly. You can even crop and annotate it.

You can bookmark unlimited web images using virtually zero disk space.

Think about that. A folder with 10,000 .repic files takes less than 10 MB. But you have instant access to 10,000 images. Wild.

More than just virtual albums

Of course, RePic isn't only about virtual albums:

  • ⚡ Blazing fast — Virtual scrolling + preloading, thousands of images buttery smooth
  • 🌐 Web albums — Drag images from any website, automatic hotlink bypass
  • ✂️ Non-destructive editing — Crop, annotate, mosaic — originals stay untouched
  • 📋 Clipboard integration — Ctrl+V to paste screenshots directly
  • 🖱️ Drag to share — Drag straight to Discord, LINE, or any app
  • 🌙 Dark & light themes — Your eyes, your choice
